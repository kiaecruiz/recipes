Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • 6 PIECES LASAGNA NOODLES
  • 1 PIECE ONION (LARGE), CHOPPED
  • 4 CLOVES GARLIC, CHOPPED
  • 1/2 POUND GROUND BEEF
  • 500 GRAMS SPAGHETTI SAUCE
  • 250 GRAMS TOMATO SAUCE
  • 1/2 TEASPOON SALT
  • 1/4 TEASPOON PEPPER
  • 5 TABLESPOONS BUTTER
  • 4 TABLESPOONS ALL-PURPOSE FLOUR
  • 2 1/2 CUP MILK
  • 1 PIECE EGG
  • 1 BLOCK EDEN ORIGINAL 160G, GRATED
  • 1 1/2 BLOCKS EDEN MELT SARAP 160G, GRATED

Directions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to instructions on packaging.
  2. In a large pan, over medium heat, sauté chopped onions and garlic then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce, tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes until thickened, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. To prepare the white sauce, melt 5 tbsp. of butter over low heat in a separate pan. Once fully melted, add 4 tbsps of flour and stir until smooth.
  5. Add milk one cup at a time, whisk until smooth. Add grated, Eden Original and egg. Cook for 20 minutes to thicken, stirring constantly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. To assemble lasagna, spread 1 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish and top with lasagna noodles until the layer is full. Then spread 1 cup of white sauce on top and add 1 cup of grated Eden Melt Sarap. Repeat these steps for another 2 layers and cover baking with with foil.
  7. Bake in the pre-heated oven for 30-45 minutes or until cheese has melted. Serve and enjoy!
